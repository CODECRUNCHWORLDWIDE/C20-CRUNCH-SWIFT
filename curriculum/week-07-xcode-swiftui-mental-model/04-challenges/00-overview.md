# Week 7 — Challenges

The exercises drill basics. **Challenges stretch you.** This one takes 90–150 minutes and produces a component you can lift directly into the "Hello, Notes" mini-project — and into your portfolio.

## Index

1. **[Challenge 1 — A card that survives the largest Dynamic Type](./challenge-01-dynamic-type-card.md)** — recreate a moderately complex card (image, title, multi-line body, trailing badge) purely with stacks and modifiers, and make it render correctly at the *largest accessibility text size* (`.accessibility5`) on iPhone SE and iPad Pro 13-inch, in light and dark. (~120 min)

Challenges are optional. If you skip them you can still pass the week. If you do them you will be measurably ahead — and this card is exactly the cell you will build for the mini-project's note list, so doing the challenge front-loads the hardest part of "Hello, Notes."

The acceptance bar for this challenge is the Phase II marker you will meet every week from here on:

```
Renders correctly on iPhone SE (3rd gen) and iPad Pro 13-inch,
in light and dark, at Dynamic Type .accessibility5 — no clipping, no truncation.
```
